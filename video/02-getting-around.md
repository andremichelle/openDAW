# 2. Getting around

Highlights, in order:
- The dashboard: where your projects live, open one or start new.
- The timeline: tracks stacked vertically, regions along time, the play button and the moving playhead.
- The device area: click a track and see its instrument and effects.
- The mixer: faders and level meters to balance things.
- Everything follows your selection — click a track and the device area and mixer channel follow it.
