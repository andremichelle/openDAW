# 1. Welcome to openDAW

Can run longer than the rest — it's the introduction and sets the tone for the whole series.

Highlights, in order:
- A finished track is already playing when the video opens — lead with the sound.
- It's a full music studio that runs in your browser. Free, nothing to install, no sign-up to start.
- Walk the four areas at a relaxed pace: the timeline where you arrange, the instruments, the effects,
  the mixer — enough that a newcomer recognises each when it comes back later in the series.
- Show a little of everything: a beat, a melody, dropping in a sample, adding an effect, moving a
  fader — keep every moment audible.
- You can save your work, back it up, and share whole projects.
- Who it's for: anyone who wants to make music, no experience needed.
- Invitation: open a blank project and follow along with the series.
