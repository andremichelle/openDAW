# openDAW video series — subjects & highlights

Prep notes for the video creator. One file per video: the **subject** and **what to show, in order**.
Wording and voice-over are yours. For musicians and beginners, not developers — keep it about making
music, no engine/tech talk. Aim 2 minutes max per video; the intro can run longer.

Ordered as a learning path. Videos 4–8 build one small track step by step.

## Order
1. `01-welcome.md` — Welcome to openDAW (intro, longer)
2. `02-getting-around.md` — Getting around
3. `03-start-a-project.md` — Start a project
4. `04-first-instrument.md` — Your first instrument and melody
5. `05-make-a-beat.md` — Make a beat
6. `06-your-samples.md` — Use your own samples
7. `07-arrange.md` — Arrange it
8. `08-automation.md` — Bring it to life with automation
9. `09-record.md` — Record your own playing
10. `10-add-effects.md` — Add effects
11. `11-mix.md` — Mix your track
12. `12-freeze-and-export.md` — Freeze & export
13. `13-visualizer.md` — Make it look good
14. `14-save-and-share.md` — Save, back up & share
15. `15-extras.md` — Handy extras

## Device spotlights
One short standalone video per device (batchable). See the `devices/` folder — each shows what the
device does with a musical before/after.
